Minimum Detectable Polarization of GRBs

We study feasibility of measuring gamma ray polarization of Gamma Ray Brusts (GRBs) using various Gamma-ray CubeSats that we construct and test using The Medium-Energy Gamma-ray Astronomy library (MEGAlib). We judge the detection of polarization performance from comparing instrument's minimum detectable polarization (MDP) for different GRBs (short or long and soft or medium or hard).
